About

About

I am Jiangyue Zeng, an undergraduate student at Peking University, working around generative AI, computer vision, and creative media.

My current interests include AIGC, diffusion models, image and video editing, controllable generation, and AI-assisted visual creation. I am especially interested in methods that make visual generation more controllable, consistent, and useful for creative workflows.

Recently, I have been exploring training-free controllable generation and video editing or relighting methods that preserve structure, temporal consistency, and visual detail.
