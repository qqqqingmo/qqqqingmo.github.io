About

Researching Generative Visual Media

I am Jiangyue Zeng, an undergraduate student at Peking University. My academic interests lie at the intersection of generative AI, computer vision, and creative media. I am particularly interested in AIGC, generative models, diffusion models, image and video editing, controllable generation, and AI-assisted visual creation.

My current interests include training-free controllable generation and video editing or relighting methods that preserve structure, temporal consistency, and visual details. I enjoy thinking about how creative systems can feel expressive while still giving users meaningful control.

Current Lens

Generative models are most exciting to me when they become tools for visual thinking, not just image production.
